Image Upload Instructions

Follow the steps below to add the images to your website.

  1. Go back to your blog site (WordPress, Wix, etc..) and add the image.
    1. For WordPress users you will have to select the “add media” button near the title of the blog post.
  2. Once you have hit “add media” simply drag and drop the image into the box or upload the image from the folder.
  3. Be sure to make this image the “featured image” of your post. Once you have selected “set feature image” you will want to “preview” the post to make sure it shows.
  4. If the image does not show, go back to to the “add media” button, find the image, and “insert into post.”
